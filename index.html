Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Click "Connect MetaMask" and approve the connection. Make sure you're on the Sepolia network.

  • If you don't have MetaMask, install it from metamask.io
  • If you're on the wrong network, MetaMask will prompt you to switch

Step 2: View Election Information

Once connected, you'll see:

  • Election name and ID
  • Voting period (start and end dates)
  • All candidates with current vote counts
  • Your voting status (whether you've already voted)

Step 3: Select Your Candidate

Click on the candidate card you want to vote for. The selected card will be highlighted.

  • You can only vote once per election
  • Make sure you select the correct candidate before voting

Step 4: Cast Your Vote (Gasless!)

Click the "Vote" button and follow these steps:

  • MetaMask will open showing a readable message like "You are voting for BJP in election #1"
  • Click "Sign" - This is FREE! No gas cost, just signing a message
  • Your vote is sent to our relayer which submits it to the blockchain
  • You pay $0 in gas fees! üéâ

Step 5: View Results

After voting, you'll see:

  • Updated vote counts for all candidates
  • Your vote confirmation
  • Real-time updates as others vote
  • Final winner after the election is finalized
